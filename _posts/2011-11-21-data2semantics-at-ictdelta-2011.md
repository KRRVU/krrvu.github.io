The [COMMIT programme](http://www.commit-nl.nl/) was officially kicked-off by [Maxime Verhagen](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maxime_Verhagen "Maxime Verhagen"), minister of [Economic Affairs, Agriculture and Innovation](http://english.minlnv.nl/pls/portal/url/page/minlnv/english) at Â the [ICTDelta 2011](http://www.ictdelta2011.nl/) event held at the [World Forum](http://www.worldforum.nl/) on November 16, in [The Hague](http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=52.0833333333,4.31666666667&spn=0.1,0.1&q=52.0833333333,4.31666666667 (The%20Hague)&t=h "The Hague").

Throughout the day, members of the Data2Semantics project manned a very busy stand in the foyer, featuring prior and current work by the project partners such as the [AIDA toolkit](http://adaptivedisclosure.org/aida/), [OpenPHACTS](http://www.openphacts.org/), [LarKC](http://www.larkc.eu/) and the [MetaLex Document Server](http://doc.metalex.eu).
